---
name: improving-presentations
description: >-
  Improves presentation content quality through storytelling, logical structure,
  audience engagement, slide design principles, and delivery techniques.
  Use when reviewing or enhancing presentation materials, restructuring slide decks,
  or preparing presentation scripts for clarity and impact.
  Covers the 5 domains: mindset/preparation (3 goals, core message design, 4 audience types),
  story/structure (1:2:1 three-act, AIDMA, situation-rain-umbrella frameworks),
  slide design, delivery (3 types of pauses, 3T principle, tension management, Q&A tactics),
  and audience engagement (listening paradigm, PUNCH principle, inclusive language).
  Focuses on content quality improvement, not technical slide generation.
  For HTML slide generation, use slidekit-create instead.
  For Google Slides API output, use generating-google-slides instead.
