description: >-
  Comprehensive training program design and facilitation methodology covering
  needs analysis (4-dimension participant analysis, stakeholder mapping),
  KSA framework (Knowledge/Skills/Attitudes),
  instructional design theory (ADDIE, TOTE, Gagné's learning outcomes, mastery learning),
  4-domain objective setting, activity catalog (opening/closing/revisit/energizer),
  training operations (instructor selection, space design, logistics, vendor management),
  brain science principles (7±2 rule, 5 long-term memory methods, 18-day habit formation),
  learner-centered facilitation (90/20/8 rule, EAT framework), online/hybrid delivery,
  skill map creation, and training material development.
  Use when designing training programs, facilitating workshops,
  creating training materials, building skill maps, planning learner-centered sessions,
  or managing training operations and logistics.
  For product behavior design using psychological triggers, use applying-behavior-design instead.
